Cheese Sandwitch

Description

The soul of cheese sandwitch is its mixture of crunchiness of its bread crust and calming essence of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chopped Vegetables
  • Cheese slice

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Make a paste of vegetables using mayo or cream and chopped vegetables
  3. Season them with flavours of your choice
  4. Spread the paste on bread and then place them in sandwitch maker with some butter applied on it.
